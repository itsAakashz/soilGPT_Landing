Alternatives to Chemical Fertilizers for Indian Farmers

Sustainable solutions to boost soil health and reduce input costs

Organic Manures: Nature's Nutrient Powerhouses

Compost Preparation

  • Mix 2 parts crop residue + 1 part cattle dung
  • Layer in 3x3x3 ft pit
  • Add Trichoderma fungi (₹50/packet)

Benefits: 30-40% urea reduction, 1-2% organic carbon boost

Vermicompost Setup

  • 10x3x2 ft beds with brick/bamboo
  • 60% cow dung + 30% crop residue
  • 1 kg earthworms per bed

Government Initiatives

  • PKVY: 50% subsidy on organic inputs
  • Neem-Coated Urea mandate
  • Pusa Decomposer at ₹20/packet

Success Story: ZBNF in Andhra Pradesh

  • Jeevamrutha weekly sprays
  • Bijamrita seed treatment
  • Coconut mulch system
