1. Land Preparation

Building a Healthy Foundation

Soil Testing

Why? Assess soil pH, nutrients (N-P-K), and organic carbon

Outcome: Customize fertilizer and lime application

Implementation

  • Use Soil Health Cards (₹50-100/test)
  • Laser leveling (₹1,000–1,500/acre)
  • Green manuring with dhaincha

Zero-Budget Natural Farming (ZBNF)

Andhra Pradesh Success Story

Steps

  1. Seed treatment with Bijamrita
  2. Soil inoculation every 15 days
  3. Mulching with dried leaves

Results

  • Yield: 4.2 tons/hectare
  • Cost reduction: ₹15k → ₹4.5k/acre

Essential Tools

  • Cono-weeders (₹2,500)
  • Seed drills (₹50-70k)
  • Solar pumps

Government Schemes

  • PM-KUSUM
  • PKVY (₹50k/ha)
  • Paramparagat Krishi
